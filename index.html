About Jagriti Shikshan Sansthan

Jagriti Shikshan Sansthan, located in Prem Nagar, Vaishali Nagar Road, Ajmer, is dedicated to providing high-quality education in various fields including pharmacy, nursing, medical laboratory technology, and more. The institution offers comprehensive programs designed to equip students with the necessary skills and knowledge to excel in their chosen careers.

Our mission is to foster a learning environment that encourages academic excellence, ethical practices, and professional growth. We believe in nurturing future leaders who will contribute positively to society and their respective fields.
